Next here are just a few of the bike tools that I have and use.

This tool is great for cutting cable houseing which is the (usualy black) layer around the break cable and shifter cable. This tool is esential for tuning up breaks and gears. It hold the break or shift cable tight so that you can tighten a bolt down on the cable which is a very difficult job if there is only one person withough this tool. The tool is used for taking apart the bike chain.
These are freewheel removers used for taking off the freewheel which is the part of the bike that has teeth on it on the rear wheel that is kind of like a ratchet because it spins freely in one direction but not the other so that when you pedal forward the bike moves. This is a chainwhip which I don't use very often but is sometimes used to tighten on a freewheel. This is a repair stand it will hold a bike up in the air to be worked on.
   
This is a truing stand used for truing wheels.
